THE PETITION

Dear Apple,

Bring art to the world and the world to art! Please add an 'Art' category to the App Store.

We believe in the power of human culture, where art plays a central role.

Apple's past and current publicity has always focused on its support for artists and the act of creation.

Yet art-centered apps are now stuck in odd categories like Lifestyle, Education, or Photos & Video.

Please show your support for creativity worldwide by adding an 'Art' category to the App Store.
